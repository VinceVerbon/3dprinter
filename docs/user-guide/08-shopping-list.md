# Shopping list

The **Shopping list** is what you take to the shop. Build it from Haspel's
built-in catalog of P2S parts and consumables, or by typing in your own items,
total it up, tick things off as you buy them, and print or save it as a PDF.

Open it from the **Shopping** entry in the top navigation.

## Adding items

There are two ways to add to the list:

- **Add from catalog** — opens a picker of the built-in catalog: official P2S
  **replacement parts** (nozzles, AMS parts, exhaust kit, spools, glue, the full
  Maker's Supply tool range, …) and **consumables** (adhesion, drying, cleaning,
  maintenance, storage). Pick an item and it's added with its name and an
  estimated price.
- **Free text** — type anything into the box (e.g. *zip ties*), set a quantity,
  and click **Add**. Free-text items have no price.

## Totals

Each line shows `quantity × unit price` where a price is known. The footer shows:

- **Estimated total** of the open (unticked) items.
- A note counting any items **without a price** (free-text items, or catalog items
  that don't carry a price) — these aren't included in the total.
- A separate line for what you've **already checked off**.

> Prices are **estimates snapshotted at the moment you added the item**. If the
> catalog price changes later, your existing list keeps the price you saw when you
> added it — so the total reflects what you expected to pay.

## Shopping with the list

- **Tick** an item's checkbox as you buy it — it greys out and moves to the
  "checked off" total.
- **Remove** an item with the trash icon.
- **Clear done** removes everything you've already ticked, in one go.

## Taking it with you

- **Print** — click **Print** to open a clean, printer-friendly version. From the
  print dialog you can send it to a printer *or* choose **Save as PDF** for an
  offline checklist on your phone.
- **On your phone** — with Haspel open on your PC and your phone on the same
  Wi-Fi, open `http://<your-pc-ip>:5173/#/shopping` to view the live list while
  you shop.
