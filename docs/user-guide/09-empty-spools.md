# Empty spools

The **Empty spools** page is a simple counter of how many empty, reusable spools
you have sitting on the shelf — ready to mount a refill on.

Open it from the **Empty spools** entry in the top navigation.

## Using it

- Press **+** when you finish a spool and keep the empty.
- Press **−** when you use an empty to mount a refill (the **−** is disabled at
  zero, so the count never goes negative).

The big number in the middle is your current free-spool count.

## Why it's here

Bambu and other brands sell filament as **refills** — filament only, no spool —
which are cheaper and less wasteful, but only useful if you have an empty spool to
wind them onto. This counter, together with the **refill** packaging count on
each [filament](02-filaments.md), helps you see at a glance whether you can actually use the
refills you have (or buy), or whether you need to keep a spool or two free.
