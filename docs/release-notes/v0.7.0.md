# Haspel v0.7.0 — 2026-05-24

## Summary

Brand-store shopping stops scraping and starts **inferring** — a per-make+model
parts list driven by the printers you own, generated by the AI provider in a
single call, with no web browsing and no rate-limiting.

## What changed and why

The v0.6.x design scraped the Bambu store and fetched each product page for
prices. That was the wrong shape:

- Prices aren't server-rendered, so they couldn't be read reliably anyway.
- Per-model store collections are mostly empty — only the P2S had a real one.
- The per-product fetch burst rate-limited the store, so repeated Updates failed.
- Worst, the claude CLI auto-browsed the store to "verify" items (5+ minutes,
  multiple turns) and timed out.

v0.7.0 replaces all of that with **AI inference tailored to your printer's make
and model**:

- The Shopping selector lists your owned **printers (brand + model)**.
- "Update store" asks the configured AI model for the spare parts, consumables,
  and add-ons that brand's store sells **for that exact model**.
- It covers the model's **multi-material unit** where it has one — Bambu AMS /
  AMS 2 Pro, Anycubic ACE / ACE Pro, Prusa MMU3, Mosaic Palette, Creality CFS —
  and **brand-specialty add-ons**: spool holders, filament dryers/warmers,
  enclosure kits, auxiliary cooling, camera/LED, etc.
- **No prices** — the store doesn't expose them to a server fetch, and you opted
  out of them.
- The call runs **tools-disabled (`--tools ""`)**, so it's a single-turn
  knowledge generation (~60–130 s) instead of an agentic web-browse. Reliable,
  no rate-limiting, on your own AI quota. Cached per `brand|model` for 24 h.

## Verified

- **Bambu Lab P2S** → AMS 2 Pro parts (hub, buffer, PTFE, cutter, desiccant) +
  Bambu add-ons (aux cooling fan, filament dryer).
- **Anycubic Kobra S1 Combo** → ACE Pro parts + dryer/wash-cure/enclosure.
- **Prusa MK4S** → no AMS (correct), Prusa-specific parts.
- UI: the selector shows "Bambu Lab P2S" and the list renders; the 30-day
  staleness prompt and the catalog picker are keyed per make+model.

## Known limitations

- **Inferred, not scraped.** Item names reflect the model's best knowledge of
  the brand store, not a live catalog read — generally accurate for major brands,
  but treat it as a tailored shortlist, not an authoritative SKU list.
- **No prices / SKUs.**
- The inference takes ~1–2 minutes per model (cached 24 h afterwards).
